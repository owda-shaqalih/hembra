We use “cookies”, a name for a specific technology used to register user preferences associated with the use of a particular website. The information stored in the cookie is used to identify you and enables us to track the pattern of behaviour of visitors to the website so that we can provide a more efficient and personalized service. You can usually modify your browser to prevent cookie use – but if you do this, the service (and the Website) may not work properly. The use of cookies by such third party advertisers or ad servers is not subject to this Policy, but is subject to their own respective privacy policies.
